📖 User Guide
🔍

Search Location

Type a destination in the bottom field and press Search or Enter. Leave the Start field empty to search from your current location, or type a campus building in the Start field to find a route between two locations.

⇅

Swap Locations

Press the ⇅ button between the two fields to swap your start and destination instantly.

👤

Find Me

Click the Find Me button to locate yourself on the map. Allow location access when prompted by your browser.

🧭

Get Directions

Search for a destination then click the Route button to get turn-by-turn directions. The route updates live as you move. Click Cancel Route to stop navigation.

🤖

Chatbot Assistant

Click the green robot button at the bottom right to open the AI assistant. Ask questions like "Where is the library?" or use the quick suggestion buttons. The chatbot can also get directions for you!

🗺️

Explore the Map

Click any emoji marker on the map to see information about that location and get directions there directly.
